Which one do we recommend?

Algebra 1 focuses on foundational concepts like linear equations, inequalities, and basic functions, making it essential for all high school students. Algebra 2 builds on these foundations with more advanced topics like logarithms, polynomials, and trigonometry. Choose Algebra 1 if your student is just starting high school math or needs to strengthen fundamentals. Choose Algebra 2 if your student has mastered Algebra 1 and is ready for more challenging concepts.
