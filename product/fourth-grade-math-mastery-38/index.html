Overview

This Scholastic workbook is a solid choice for grade 4 math practice. The book provides comprehensive coverage of multiplication and division, fractions and decimals, measurement conversions, and geometry, making it ideal for students who need structured practice to build confidence. Each lesson progresses logically, with clear explanations followed by plenty of practice problems that reinforce key concepts. The workbook includes answer keys in the back, which makes it easy for parents to check work without having to solve every problem themselves. This format works particularly well for kids who need consistent daily practice or for homeschool families who want a curriculum that aligns with Common Core standards. The straightforward approach focuses on building skills gradually, so students don't get overwhelmed. While the black-and-white interior may not be as visually appealing as some workbooks, it eliminates distractions and keeps the focus on learning. This workbook is especially effective for students who struggle with math because it breaks down concepts into manageable steps.
