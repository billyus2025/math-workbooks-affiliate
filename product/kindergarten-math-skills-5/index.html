Overview

This Carson workbook is a solid choice for grade k math practice. The book provides comprehensive coverage of counting, number recognition, shapes, patterns, and basic addition, making it ideal for students who need structured practice to build confidence. Each lesson progresses logically, with clear explanations followed by plenty of practice problems that reinforce key concepts.
