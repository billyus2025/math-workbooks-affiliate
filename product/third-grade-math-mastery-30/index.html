Overview

The Scholastic grade 3 math workbook offers a comprehensive approach to math practice that many families find effective. The book covers multiplication, division, fractions, and geometry, providing thorough coverage of all the concepts students need to master. Each lesson includes clear explanations followed by practice problems of varying difficulty, so students can start with basics and work their way up.
